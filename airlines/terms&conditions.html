Terms & Conditions

DEPOSITS:

50% of the total amount of the packages should be paid at the time of booking.

FINAL PAYMENTS:

The final payment is due in full, 05 weeks before the arrival date to Kathmandu. The company reserves the right to cancel any reservation for which the fee is not received when due.

CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS:

Cancellation must be notified in writing to us, which booking has been made.

* Cancellation 20 days before Arrival---10% of Total Tour Cost.
* Cancellation 10 Days before Arrival---30% of Total Tour Cost.
* Cancellation, within 7 days of Arrival---50% of Total Tour Cost.
* Cancellation upon arrival in Kathmandu---100% Tour cost

There will be no refunds if any client leaves a trip for any reason whatsoever either voluntarily or involuntarily, after the trip has begun. No refunds will be made for any meals, accommodation of services not utilized.

INSURANCE:

Passengers must be protected by insurance that covers cancellation, accident, health, emergency evacuation and loss of damage to baggage and personal effects.

HEALTH:

Good health and physical fitness are required on all trekking trips. A physical examination is recommend for all participants before arrive to Kathmandu.

PASSPORT & VISAS:

All passengers must be in possession of al valid passport and obtain a Nepal Visa, before coming to Nepal or on arrival at Kathmandu Airport.

RESPONSIBILITY:

The company acts as agents for transport companies, hotel and other contractors and shall not held liable for any injury, damage, loss, delay or irregularity that may be occasioned for any reason.
The airlines concerned are not to be held responsibilities for any act, or event during the time passengers are not on board their planes.


The company reserves the right to cancel any tour prior to departure. In such a case, full refunds of all payments will constitute full settlement with the passenger. Some of the trips such as rafting and trekking are based on minimum group sizes and the right is reserved to surcharge passenger if this group size is not achieved. The company reserves the right to decline, to accept, or to retain any person, as a member of any party any time.


All above terms and conditions are subject to laws of the country involved.
